7.17.24 is a short film documenting my first few weeks in college. i took all the shots with a panasonic lumix g100d camera, and edited the video using adobe premiere pro cc. the post production took around 10 hours total.

the goal was to improve my color grading with more dynamic shots. through this video, i believe i got a better grasp on color theory. i put some of my favorite frames utilizing my knowledge on color theory above.

to learn more, check it out on insta or youtube.
